User Data Deletion Instructions

Agent HQ · Meta app: Agent (App ID 1361689412072064)
Effective date: July 9, 2026 · Last updated: July 9, 2026

Meta requires apps to provide clear instructions for deleting user data. This page explains how to request deletion of data associated with our Meta integration and Agent HQ systems.

Quick request

Email support@struckgold.co with subject line Data deletion request.

1. What this covers

Data that may have been stored in Agent HQ or related systems as a result of:

  • Messaging, comments, or leads received via Facebook/Instagram professional accounts we operate;
  • Customer support cases or notes that reference Meta-sourced conversations;
  • Technical logs that include user identifiers from Meta APIs.

Advertising performance metrics that are aggregated business analytics (not tied to identifying you personally) may be retained as business records.

2. How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@struckgold.co.
  2. Use subject: Data deletion request.
  3. Include:
    • Your name;
    • The email, phone, Facebook/Instagram name, or Page you used to contact us (if known);
    • Approximate dates of interaction (if known);
    • Whether you want deletion of support/message records related to you.
  4. We will confirm receipt and complete deletion of personal data we control within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law (for example fraud prevention or legal holds).

3. What we will delete

  • Message/comment/lead content stored in Agent HQ that identifies you;
  • Support ticket notes and conversation history we hold that are primarily about you;
  • Associated personal identifiers in our operational databases where feasible.

4. What we may retain

  • Records we must keep for legal, tax, or dispute reasons;
  • Anonymized or aggregated analytics that no longer identify you;
  • Backups for a limited period until rotated, after which data is purged.

5. Meta platform controls

You can also manage data and permissions directly with Meta:

  • Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites (remove apps where applicable);
  • Instagram settings for connected experiences;
  • Meta’s Help Center tools for downloading or deleting your Meta account data.

Removing our App or stopping messaging does not automatically delete historical business records we already stored; use the email process above for that.

6. Operators / system users

If you are an internal operator and need credentials or business data removed from Agent HQ, contact the same email with subject Operator data deletion. System-user tokens can be revoked in Meta Business Settings and removed from Agent HQ connectors.
