Privacy Policy

Agent HQ · Meta app: Agent (App ID 1361689412072064)
Operator: Struck Gold (and affiliated businesses operated by the same owner)
Effective date: July 9, 2026 · Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Agent HQ (“the App,” “we,” “us”) handles information when the App connects to Meta Platforms, Inc. services (including Facebook Graph API and Marketing API) and when people interact with content or messaging we manage through those integrations.

1. Who we are

Agent HQ is an internal business operations platform used to run AI-assisted workflows for ecommerce and related operations (customer support, advertising reporting, content, and internal tooling). The Meta developer app named Agent is used so our systems can access business assets we own or administer (for example advertising accounts, pages, and Instagram professional accounts linked to our business).

Contact for privacy requests: support@struckgold.co

Website: https://struckgold.co

2. Scope

This policy covers:

  • Data processed by the Agent HQ software and the Meta “Agent” app integration;
  • Business data retrieved from Meta APIs for accounts we control (ads, pages, insights, messaging where authorized);
  • End-user data that may appear in those APIs when people interact with our Pages, ads, or Instagram professional accounts.

It does not replace the privacy policy of struckgold.co storefront checkout or other third-party processors (Shopify, email providers, etc.), which have their own policies.

3. Information we process

Depending on configuration and permissions granted to the App, we may process:

  • Business account data from Meta: ad account metadata, campaigns, ad sets, ads, insights, spend, performance metrics, and related IDs;
  • Page / Instagram professional data where authorized: page metadata, posts, comments, insights, and messaging content required to operate support or marketing workflows;
  • Lead and form data if lead ads or forms are used;
  • Technical logs needed to operate the App: API errors, timestamps, agent run logs, and connection status;
  • Operator data: credentials and tokens stored to keep the integration connected (system user tokens, account IDs).

We do not sell personal information. We do not use Meta data to build independent consumer profiles for sale to third parties.

4. How we use information

  • Operate internal AI agents that report on and help manage advertising and related operations;
  • Provide customer support and messaging workflows when those features are enabled;
  • Improve reliability (debugging failed API calls, reconnecting integrations);
  • Comply with law and platform terms.

5. Legal bases / legitimate interests

We process this information to operate our business, fulfill customer requests, measure and improve marketing, and secure our systems. Where required, we rely on consent obtained through Meta’s tools (for example lead forms) or other lawful bases under applicable law.

6. Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Meta Platforms, as needed to use their APIs under Meta’s terms;
  • Infrastructure providers that host Agent HQ or store logs (under contractual confidentiality);
  • Service providers we use to run the business (e.g. ecommerce, email, analytics) when a workflow requires it;
  • Authorities when required by law.

We do not sell or rent personal information.

7. Retention

We retain business operational data (including API-derived metrics and support case notes) for as long as needed to run the business, meet legal/accounting requirements, or resolve disputes. Integration credentials are retained until disconnected or rotated. Logs may be retained for a limited period for security and debugging.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect tokens and operational data, including access controls on Agent HQ and restricted use of system-user tokens for server-side automation. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Your choices and rights

If you are an end user of our public Facebook/Instagram presence and want to exercise privacy rights (access, correction, deletion) regarding data we hold:

  • Email support@struckgold.co with subject line Privacy request;
  • See also our Data Deletion Instructions.

You can also use Meta’s settings and tools to manage ad preferences, page follows, and messaging permissions.

10. Children’s privacy

Agent HQ and the Agent app are not directed to children under 13 (or the age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this App.

11. International users

We primarily operate in the United States. If you access our services from other regions, your information may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate.

12. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes notice of the revised policy for operators of the App.
